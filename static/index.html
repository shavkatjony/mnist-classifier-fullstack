How It Processes
  1. Your drawing is scaled to 28×28 pixels and flattened to a 784-dim vector
  2. Each hidden layer applies a linear transformation (matrix multiply + bias)
  3. ReLU activation sets negative values to zero — adding non-linearity
  4. Dropout randomly zeros 20% of neurons during training to prevent overfitting
  5. The output layer applies Softmax to produce probabilities that sum to 1
